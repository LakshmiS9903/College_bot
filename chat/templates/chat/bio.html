Biomedical and Robotics Engineering Course

Course Overview

This course integrates biomedical science with robotics engineering, preparing students to design and develop cutting-edge medical devices and robotic systems that revolutionize healthcare and enhance patient outcomes.

Course Details

Description: This course bridges the fields of biomedical engineering and robotics, focusing on developing innovative solutions such as prosthetics, medical robots, and automated diagnostic systems. Students will acquire skills in biomechanics, medical imaging, and robotic systems design.

Duration: 4 years

Price: 4.5 Lakhs

Instructor(s): Dr. Y, Dr. Z, Prof. AA, Dr. BB, Dr. CC, Prof. DD
