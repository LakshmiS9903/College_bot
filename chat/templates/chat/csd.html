Computer Science and Design Engineering Course

Course Overview

This course blends core Computer Science concepts with principles of design, focusing on developing innovative solutions that integrate technology and creative design. Students will learn programming, user-centric design, and modern software development practices.

Course Details

Description: This interdisciplinary course combines computer science fundamentals with design principles, teaching students how to create intuitive and aesthetically pleasing software solutions that prioritize user experience and innovative technology.

Duration: 4 years

Price: 4.2 Lakhs

Prerequisites: Basic programming experience, an understanding of design concepts, and mathematical foundations.

Instructor(s): Dr. S, Dr. T, Prof. U, Dr. V, Dr. W, Prof. X
