Civil and Environmental Engineering Course

Course Overview

This course offers a comprehensive study of Civil and Environmental Engineering, focusing on the design, development, and maintenance of sustainable infrastructure, as well as understanding environmental impacts and solutions.

Course Details

Description: This course bridges civil engineering and environmental science, focusing on sustainable practices, structural design, water resources management, and ecological impact analysis. Students will gain knowledge to innovate and implement solutions for modern infrastructure while ensuring environmental preservation.

Duration: 4 years

Price: 3.5 Lakhs

Prerequisites: Background in physics, mathematics, and basic engineering concepts.

Instructor(s): Dr. M, Dr. N, Prof. O, Dr. P, Dr. Q, Prof. R
